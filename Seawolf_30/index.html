Testimony

I grew up in a broken home like most. I spent many of my younger years feeling unlovable and alone. But then God… When I was fifteen years old, God met me right where I was, speaking to me in an undeniable way. In that moment, my whole life changed. I understood that, whether I deserved it or not, there was a God who loved me and chose me as His own. I have done my best to serve Him and follow His will for my life ever since.
