Join the FaithForge Gaming Raid Train

The FaithForge Gaming Raid Train is a quarterly Christian gaming community event designed to support current, new, and up-and-coming streamers through shared visibility, live raids, real encouragement, and active community participation.

During each raid train, our community comes together to back FaithForge creators by showing up in chat, following channels, sharing streams, participating in raids, and helping each featured streamer feel supported as they grow. This is one of the ways FaithForge Gaming helps new streamers build momentum and become part of a stronger Christian creator community.

Streamers who want to be part of future FaithForge raid train events can sign up through our Discord community, where we organize quarterly event details, creator participation, lineup updates, and community involvement.

About the Event

How the FaithForge Raid Train Works

The FaithForge Gaming Raid Train is a recurring quarterly event created to strengthen our Christian gaming community and actively support FaithForge streamers. Our goal is to help both established creators and newer streamers grow through shared exposure, meaningful encouragement, and strong community support.

Throughout the event, viewers move from one featured streamer to the next, helping boost engagement, welcome new audiences, and create a more connected community experience. Rather than letting streamers grow alone, the FaithForge community comes together to back the current lineup and help rising creators get noticed.

This page makes it easy to follow the live lineup, see who is currently streaming, and know where the next stop on the raid train is headed. It is built to help viewers support the event in real time while also helping new visitors understand what the FaithForge Raid Train is all about.

If you are a streamer who wants to participate in a future raid train, the best place to get started is inside the FaithForge Gaming Discord. That is where creators can connect with the community, find sign-up details, stay informed about upcoming quarterly events, and learn how to become part of the FaithForge creator lineup.

FAQ

FaithForge Raid Train FAQ

What is the FaithForge Raid Train?

The FaithForge Raid Train is a quarterly FaithForge Gaming event where the community supports Christian streamers by joining live streams, encouraging creators, and raiding from one channel to the next.

How do streamers join the raid train?

Streamers can join by becoming part of the FaithForge Gaming Discord community and following the sign-up information shared there for each upcoming quarterly raid train event.

Who is the raid train for?

The raid train is for current FaithForge creators, new streamers, and up-and-coming Christian streamers who want more support, more visibility, and a stronger community around their content.

How can the community support the event?

Community members can support the raid train by watching streams, chatting, following, sharing, participating in raids, and helping encourage each featured creator during the event.
