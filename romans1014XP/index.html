About Jose

Hey, I’m Jose — husband, dad, pastor, lifelong gamer, WWE fan, and community builder. My faith shapes how I live, lead, and listen. I serve as a pastor and chaplain and lead Life On Mission Church, a hybrid micro-church community built on relationships, not routines. But here? I’m just Romans... gaming, talking, listening well, and building something real.

Testimony & Mission

I was raised Catholic in the South Bronx but drifted from my faith as a young adult. After years of pursuing success while feeling spiritually empty, and walking through some dark times, God began drawing me back to Himself. In 2016, I recommitted my life to Christ and experienced the hope, purpose, and transformation that only He can provide.

Today, I serve as a pastor, chaplain, and content creator. Through gaming, ministry, and community, my mission is simple: Walk with Christ. Build Community. Live on Mission.
