Testimony

I wasn't raised a Christian, but throughout my life I always said I was because I just thought that was the right answer. It wasn't until my senior year in highschool that I really grasped Christianity for what it was when I was presented with the Gospel for the first time. There are quite a few moments that lead me to believe Salvation occurred shortly after, but my understanding of Jesus's finished work on the cross only grew as time went on. What I know now is God saved me when I didn't deserve it, and when I didn't earn it, purely by my trust in Jesus. He can save you too if you trust in Jesus for eternal salvation from your sins. My hope is that his death and resurrection is enough for you, as it was for me. Because without Jesus we, I, am without hope. "Come to me, all who labor and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest. Take my yoke upon you, and learn from me, for I am gentle and lowly in heart, and you will find rest for your souls. For my yoke is easy, and my burden is light." -Matthew 11:28-30
