Matthew Jakab

Founder — G0dblessing

I’m the founder of FaithForge Gaming, and my heart is to build a Christ-centered gaming community where people uplift, sharpen, and encourage one another. I’m passionate about construction, remodeling, and creating things—whether that’s a piece of furniture, a renovation project, or a new server experience for our community.

I play a variety of games but mostly FPS in the late evenings once the house is quiet. My story isn’t perfect—I strayed from the Lord during my college years. But when I wanted out of the pit I was in, I turned back to God. In His grace, He led me to my amazing wife, and now we serve in our church weekly while raising our two beautiful daughters.

At FaithForge I run servers, organize community events, and lead our Discord Bible study. I love helping people and genuinely enjoy walking alongside others in gaming and in faith. Welcome to FaithForge Gaming—let’s grow together and sharpen one another.
