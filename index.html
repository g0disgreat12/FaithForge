What Is the FaithForge Raid Train?

The FaithForge Raid Train is a quarterly Christian gaming event where our community moves from one livestream to the next to support FaithForge creators.

Each streamer gets a scheduled time slot, and when their stream ends, the community raids into the next channel. Our goal is to help current, new, and up-and-coming Christian streamers feel supported, grow their audience, and build stronger fellowship through gaming.

FaithForge Content Standard

Raid Train streams should remain clean, community-friendly, and aligned with FaithForge Gaming values.

  • Clean and respectful content
  • Community-friendly chat environment
  • FaithForge-approved games or content style
  • Encouraging, welcoming, and family-conscious atmosphere

Want to Stream in a Future Raid Train?

Streamers who want to be part of future FaithForge Raid Train events can sign up through our Discord community. That is where we organize quarterly event details, creator participation, lineup updates, and community involvement.

FaithForge Raid Train FAQ

Quick answers for viewers, streamers, and community members.

What is the FaithForge Raid Train?

The FaithForge Raid Train is a quarterly FaithForge Gaming event where the community supports Christian streamers by joining live streams, encouraging creators, and raiding from one channel to the next.

How do streamers join?

Streamers can join by becoming part of the FaithForge Gaming Discord community and following the sign-up information shared there for each upcoming quarterly raid train event.

Who is the raid train for?

The raid train is for current FaithForge creators, new streamers, and up-and-coming Christian streamers who want more support, visibility, and community around their content.

How can viewers support the event?

Viewers can support the raid train by watching streams, chatting, following, sharing, participating in raids, and helping encourage each featured creator during the event.
