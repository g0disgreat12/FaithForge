Founder of Blessing Boat

Blessing Boat is a Christian streaming community event created to help new, growing, and non-affiliate Christian streamers connect with other creators. The heart behind it is simple: bring streamers together, encourage one another, share audiences through raids, and build friendships that can turn into real relationships.

Events are held monthly and are open to Christian streamers who want to participate, collaborate, and grow together. Not every application can be approved, so interested creators are encouraged to apply early when spots open.

About Warrior

Warrior is a gamer through and through. His first video game was Pong, so yes — he has been gaming since before a lot of today’s streamers were even in diapers. He mostly streams variety games, life simulation games, racing with friends, indie titles, free games, and community picks.

Old-School Gamer

He has grown up with video games and brings a relaxed, classic gamer feel to his streams.

Faith First

Warrior accepted Christ when he was young and wants his stream to show Jesus through conversation, prayer, and kindness.

Family Man

He is a husband, a dad, and someone who has seen God’s faithfulness through years of family challenges and victories.

Community Builder

He loves meeting people from around the world and helping Christian creators connect through Blessing Boat.

Testimony & Heart for Ministry

Warrior gives God credit for saving him from disaster in high school, college, and marriage. He has seen God provide protection, a home, transportation, work, a faithful marriage, and relationships with other creators. His son is a miracle, having lived far beyond what was expected, and Warrior’s story is marked by gratitude for the Lord’s care through hard seasons.

He is honest about not being perfect and still having struggles, but his hope is fixed on eternity with God. His stream is a place where people can laugh, talk, ask questions, receive prayer, and be reminded that they matter.

Favorite Bible Verse
