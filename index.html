FaithForge Gaming is a biblically-based Christian gaming community where players come together to share faith, fellowship, and fun. We believe gaming can be more than just entertainment — it can be a forge that strengthens faith, builds character, and unites believers around the world.

We stand firmly on the Word of God and the teachings of Jesus Christ. Our community commits itself to the values and lessons of the New Testament, living out our faith not just in life, but also in our time online together.

Our motto comes from Proverbs 27:17 — "As iron sharpens iron, so one person sharpens another."
